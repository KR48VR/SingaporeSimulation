    Population and land-area change

    The charts anchor the simulation with known census and official modern data. Earlier centuries remain illustrative, while population after 1950 and land area after 1960 are tied to official modern series and interpolated between selected points for the timeline stops.

    Population uses historical census points and modern official data. The plot uses a log scale because growth is large over time.
    Land area remains near the natural-island baseline before 1960, then follows the official modern series as reclamation expands Singapore.

    Sources and caveats

    What changed in this version

    This version switches the visual reference from a stylized map to the OneMap LandLot cadastral basemap. Because present-day parcels are being used to teach a long historical timeline, the historical overlays are intentionally generalized and should not be read as exact historical lot boundaries or exact shorelines for earlier centuries.

    QA notes and sources

    Accuracy review in this build

    Important caveat: the OneMap LandLot basemap shows current cadastral parcels. It is useful for present-day spatial reference, but it is not a historical cadastre. Historical settlement, reclamation, and rail layers are generalized teaching overlays, not survey-grade boundaries or track alignments.
    • Replaced the previous single generic MRT strokes with separate NSL, EWL, NEL, CCL, DTL, and TEL polylines.
    • Split rail display into opening-year segments, so post-1960 year-by-year stops no longer show the whole network too early.
    • Added route station dots and labels for active rail lines; CCL6 is not drawn before the 2025 endpoint because its loop closure is slated for 2026.
    • Kept population before 1819 qualitative to avoid pretending there are exact census figures.
